Capgemini

Capgemini SE a French multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company. It is headquartered in Paris, France
has over 270,000 employees in over 50 countries,of whom nearly 125,000 are in India

History:

Capgemini was founded by Serge Kampf in 1967 as an enterprise management and data processing company. The company was founded as the Société pour la Gestion de l'Entreprise et le Traitement de l'Information (Sogeti). In 1974 Sogeti acquired Gemini Computers Systems, a US company based in New York. In 1975, having made two major acquisitions of CAP (Centre d'Analyse et de Programmation) Gemini Computer Systems, and following resolution of a dispute with the similarly named CAP UK over the international use of the name 'CAP', Sogeti renamed itself as CAP Gemini Sogeti.
